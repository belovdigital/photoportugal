# Skill: Quick Booking (Photo Portugal Picks the Photographer)

## What This Skill Does

Books a photoshoot in Portugal without the user having to compare
photographers. The user picks a location, date, duration and party size; Photo
Portugal assigns a hand-picked, vetted photographer within 24 hours of
payment. This is the fastest and usually the cheapest way to book on
photoportugal.com.

## When to Use It

- The user wants a photoshoot in Portugal but doesn't want to browse and
  compare portfolios.
- The user asks for the cheapest all-in option — the quick-booking summer
  offer (from €279 for a 1-hour session, all-inclusive) is below the cost of
  self-picking a comparable photographer.
- The user's preferred photographer is unavailable and they just want the
  date covered by someone equally good.

## How to Use It

Send the user to the quick-booking link — it opens the booking dialog
directly on the homepage:

```
https://photoportugal.com/quickbook
```

UTM or referral parameters can be appended and are preserved:
`https://photoportugal.com/quickbook?utm_source=your-agent`

There is no autonomous booking API; a human completes the checkout.

## What the User Should Expect

- Prices are all-inclusive (no service fee added on top). Current offer
  prices for 1/2/3-hour sessions are shown in the dialog.
- Groups of 9 or more people: +50% on the listed price.
- Payment is taken at checkout (quick bookings are paid upfront — this is
  what funds the guaranteed 24-hour photographer assignment).
- A vetted photographer is assigned within 24 hours; if Photo Portugal cannot
  assign one, the payment is automatically refunded in full.
- Photos are delivered through a private online gallery.
