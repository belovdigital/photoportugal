## REMOVED KEYWORDS (for reference)

The following categories were cut from this file:

- **Informational/Instagram keywords** (35+ keywords like "best photo spots lisbon", "instagram spots porto", "most instagrammable places") -- These drive traffic but not bookings. Keep targeting them in blog posts for top-of-funnel, but they are not in this money keyword file.
- **Wedding photographer keywords** (8 keywords like "lisbon wedding photographer", "algarve wedding photographer", "destination wedding portugal") -- Not core service. High competition from wedding directories.
- **Sub-50/mo keywords** (20+ keywords like "guimaraes photoshoot", "obidos photographer", "rainy day photoshoot lisbon") -- Not worth dedicated optimization effort at this stage.
- **Non-core services** (keywords like "headshot photographer lisbon", "surf photographer ericeira", "portugal photo walk", "portugal photography tours") -- Not what we sell.
- **Too-broad keywords** ("portugal vacation tips", "portugal photos") -- Zero booking intent.
- **Honeymoon itinerary / romantic places** (informational intent, not photographer-booking intent) -- Kept honeymoon PHOTOGRAPHER keywords, removed pure travel planning keywords.
